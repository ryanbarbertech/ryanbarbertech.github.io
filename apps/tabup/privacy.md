# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 20, 2026

Thank you for using TabUp.

## Information Collection

TabUp does not collect, store, or share any personal information or usage data.

## iCloud Sync

If iCloud Sync is enabled on your device, TabUp may store your data in your personal iCloud account so it can sync across your Apple devices. This data is managed by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. TabUp does not have access to your iCloud data.

## Third-Party Services

TabUp does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect your personal information.

## Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you choose to use iCloud Sync, your data is also stored in your personal iCloud account to enable syncing between your devices.

## Children's Privacy

TabUp does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date above.
